## What is FurEverHomes?
FurEverHomes is more than just a pet adoption website—it's a cozy corner of the internet where pets and people come together to create lifelong friendships. Whether you're looking for a playful pup, a cuddly kitten, or a quirky hamster, we've got a furry friend waiting just for you!

## Why Adopt With Us?
- **Adorable Profiles:** Scroll through cute photos and fun facts about each pet.
- **Easy Adoption Process:** Our forms are simple, friendly, and designed to help you find your perfect companion.
- **Community Vibes:** Join a community of animal lovers, share your adoption stories, and spread the love!

## How It Works
1. Browse our available pets (warning: extreme cuteness ahead).

## 🚀 How Does FurEverHomes Work?
FurEverHomes is a full-stack web application designed to make pet adoption easy and enjoyable:

- **Home Page:** Start your journey with a warm welcome and easy navigation.
- **Browse Pets:** Explore available cats, dogs, and hamsters with detailed profiles and adorable images.
- **Adoption Forms:** Fill out simple forms to express interest in your chosen pet.
- **Volunteer & Donate:** Get involved by signing up to volunteer or making a donation to support our furry friends.
- **Contact & About Us:** Learn more about our mission and reach out with any questions.

All data is securely managed in our database, and the site is designed to be fast, friendly, and fun!

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **Languages:** Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Frameworks & Libraries:**
	- Frontend: Bootstrap, jQuery, Animate.css, Owl Carousel
	- Backend: Python (custom scripts for logic)
- **Database:** SQL (see `db/FurEverHomes_db1.sql`)
- **Static Assets:** Custom images, icons, and styles in the `static/` folder
- **Templates:** HTML pages in the `templates/` folder for each feature

## 💡 Project Structure
- `kevin.py`, `shradha.py`, `simran.py`: Python scripts for backend logic
- `db/`: Database files
- `static/`: CSS, JS, images, fonts, uploads
- `templates/`: HTML pages for all site features
