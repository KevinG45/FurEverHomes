Volunteer

Welcome to Furever Home's Volunteer Hub!

At Furever homes, we believe every pet deserves a loving home, and we can’t do it without the help of our incredible volunteers. Whether you’re passionate about animals, want to give back to your community, or are simply looking for a rewarding experience, we have a place for you.

Why Volunteer with us?

  • Impact Lives: Your support helps homeless animals receive the care, love, and attention they need to thrive.
  • Build Skills and Knowledge: From animal care to event planning, volunteering offers hands-on experience in areas that can enhance your personal and professional growth.
  • Be Part of a Community: Connect with other passionate animal lovers and make lasting friendships.
  • Flexible Opportunities: Whether you have a few hours a week or a day a month, we offer roles that fit your schedule.

Volunteer Opportunities

At Furever Homes, everyone can contribute! Help animals directly through Hands-On Care by walking, feeding, grooming, or socializing them. Join our Foster Care Program to provide temporary homes, or assist at Adoption Events, matching pets with families. Prefer behind-the-scenes work? Support us with administrative tasks, tech, or online outreach. Love driving? Join the Transport Team to ensure pets reach appointments and foster homes safely.

Who Can Volunteer?

We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you’re a student looking for community service hours, a professional seeking meaningful ways to give back, or a retiree wanting to stay active, we have a place for you. Some roles may have age or physical requirements, but we strive to accommodate everyone.

How to Get Started?

  1. Complete Our Online Application: Start here.
  2. Attend a Volunteer Orientation: Learn about our mission, policies, and available roles.
  3. Choose Your Role: We’ll help you find the perfect fit based on your skills and interests.
  4. Start Making a Difference: Jump in and start creating a positive impact in the lives of our animals!
